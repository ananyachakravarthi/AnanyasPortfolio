Long Form


Accessibility

As someone passionate about inclusivity in science, I’m working on a project to make bioinformatics tools accessible to everyone. I focus on designing data visuals, like graphs and images, with alternative text and colorblind-friendly color schemes. I also prioritize creating user-friendly interfaces that work seamlessly with assistive devices, making sure that clear, straightforward language is used throughout. Cross-platform compatibility is a big part of my vision, ensuring that these tools are functional on all devices and accessible through assistive technologies​

Ten-Page Spread

This project was my creative exploration of designing a vibrant, visually engaging mock magazine spread titled "Culinary Passport." The spread takes readers on a cultural journey through food, highlighting the cuisines and traditions of countries like Greece, Spain, and Japan. I focused on combining bold visuals with compelling narratives to evoke the essence of each region. This project allowed me to experiment with layout design, typography, and color schemes, blending storytelling with aesthetic appeal to create an immersive experience.

UX Design Branding Project

This project was a deep dive into reimagining the identity of Giant Eagle. I conducted user-centered research, including interviews and feedback sessions, to understand how design elements like color palettes, fonts, logos, and slogans resonate with diverse audiences. Drawing from participant insights, I refined the brand’s visuals and messaging to emphasize sustainability, community, and freshness. The final design features a modern eagle logo, symbolizing growth and vitality, paired with the slogan, “Where Community and Vitality Take Flight.” This work represents my commitment to creating thoughtful, inclusive, and impactful brand identities​
